In a candid display of humility and resilience, Shannon and Priyanka, the dynamic duo behind the popular news app Volv, graced Zurich with their presence at the recent "Fuckup Night". The event, known for its unconventional approach to celebrating business failures as stepping stones to success, was the perfect platform for the founders to share their journey.

Shannon and Priyanka, who have revolutionized the way we consume news with their bite-sized content, spoke openly about the challenges they faced on their path to stardom. They shared anecdotes of early setbacks, from struggling to secure funding to dealing with technical glitches that threatened to derail their vision.

Despite these hurdles, the founders' unwavering belief in their product and their commitment to delivering news in a concise, digestible format ultimately led to the creation of Volv. Today, the app stands as a testament to their perseverance, transforming the way millions of users worldwide stay informed.

The event was a hit among Zurich's tech community, with many attendees expressing admiration for Shannon and Priyanka's honesty and resilience. Their story served as a powerful reminder that success often comes after a series of failures, and that the journey to stardom is rarely a straight path.
